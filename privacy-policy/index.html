This Privacy Policy describes how HaiMusic ("App"), developed by HaldarAi, collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application. By using HaiMusic, you agree to the practices described here.

01

Interpretation & Definitions

Interpretation: Words with capitalised initials have meanings defined below. These definitions apply regardless of whether they appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

App
HaiMusic — the mobile application provided by HaldarAi.
Developer / Company
HaldarAi, the entity responsible for the development and maintenance of HaiMusic.
Personal Data
Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
Usage Data
Data collected automatically when you interact with the App (e.g. session duration, features accessed).
You / User
Any individual who installs or uses the HaiMusic application.
02

Data We Collect

Personal & Usage Data

We collect limited data to provide and improve the HaiMusic experience:

Usage Data
Songs played, playlists created, features accessed, and session duration — used to personalise your experience.
Device Information
Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
Analytics Data
Anonymous, aggregated data about app performance and crash reports — never linked to you personally.

App Permissions

HaiMusic requests the following Android permissions. Each is used solely for the purpose stated:

🌐
INTERNET
Streams music and communicates with our servers for online playback and content delivery.
📶
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
Checks connectivity status so the app can decide between streaming and cached content.
▶️
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK
Keeps music playing uninterrupted when the app is in the background or the screen is off.
🔒
WAKE_LOCK
Prevents the device from sleeping during playback to ensure a continuous audio experience.
🎙️
RECORD_AUDIO
Used only when you actively engage voice search or voice commands. Audio is processed for your request only — never for surveillance.
🔊
MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS
Adjusts volume levels and output device settings to optimise your listening experience.
🔔
POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Sends playback controls and important app updates to your notification shade.
⚙️
DYNAMIC_RECEIVER_NOT_EXPORTED_PERMISSION
An internal app component permission. It does not access any user data.
03

How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve HaiMusic:

Service Delivery
To provide core functionality — music streaming, offline playback, and audio effects.
Personalisation
To tailor recommendations, Quick Picks, and Recently Played based on your listening habits.
App Improvement
To analyse usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve performance and new features.
Notifications
To send playback controls and relevant app updates (with your permission).
Security & Compliance
To detect and prevent misuse, technical issues, or fraudulent activity.
We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.
04

Retention Periods

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Policy — including meeting legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements.

Usage and analytics data is retained in anonymised form for up to 24 months for performance analysis. When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

05

Data Transfers

HaiMusic is developed in West Bengal, India and operates under Indian data protection law. If you access the App from outside India, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in India, where data protection standards may differ from your country.

By using the App, you consent to this transfer. We take steps to ensure any such transfer is handled securely and in accordance with applicable law.

06

Your Rights & Deletion

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access & Update
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, or ask us to correct inaccurate information.
Deletion
Request that we delete your personal data. We will comply unless we are legally required to retain it.
Objection
Object to our processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
Restriction
Request that we limit how we process your personal data.
Portability
Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at business.tizongaming@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Disclosure of Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share it only in the following limited situations:

Service Providers
Trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating the App (e.g. analytics, crash reporting). They access data only as needed to perform their tasks and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
Legal Requirements
When required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect our legal rights, user safety, or the public.
Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. You will be notified in advance.
08

Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data — including encryption in transit and restricted access controls.

⚠️ No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you suspect a security breach affecting your data, please contact us immediately at business.tizongaming@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

HaiMusic is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data to us, please contact us immediately. We will promptly delete any such data from our systems upon verification.
